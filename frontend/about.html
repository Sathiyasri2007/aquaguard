About AquaGuard

Our Mission

To protect communities in Rural Northeast India through AI-powered water quality monitoring and early disease detection, ensuring access to safe drinking water for all.

What We Do

AquaGuard is a comprehensive water safety solution that combines artificial intelligence, computer vision, and real-time monitoring to detect water contamination and predict water-borne diseases before they spread.

Our system analyzes water sample images using advanced CNN models, identifies contamination indicators, and provides instant risk assessments. We work closely with NGOs, health departments, and community workers to ensure rapid response to contamination threats.

Why It Matters

Water-borne diseases remain a significant health challenge in rural areas. Early detection and rapid response can save lives and prevent disease outbreaks. Our AI-powered system provides 24/7 monitoring with 90%+ accuracy, making water safety accessible to remote communities.

Our Impact

Since launch, we've monitored over 1,991 locations across Northeast India, detected contamination in high-risk areas, and enabled rapid response from health officials. Our system has helped prevent potential disease outbreaks and improved water safety awareness in communities.

Technology

We use state-of-the-art deep learning models, computer vision techniques, and geographic information systems to provide comprehensive water quality analysis. Our platform integrates with existing health infrastructure to ensure seamless coordination and response.
