Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how AquaGuard collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better water monitoring services:

  • Account information (name, email, role)
  • Water sample images and location data
  • Analysis results and risk assessments
  • Usage data and system interactions

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide water quality analysis and disease predictions
  • Send alerts to health officials and NGOs
  • Improve our AI models and detection accuracy
  • Generate reports and track contamination patterns
  • Communicate important updates and safety information

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure authentication with JWT tokens
  • Password hashing and protection
  • Regular security audits

Data Sharing

We share data only when necessary for public health:

  • Health departments and NGOs (for emergency response)
  • Authorized community health workers
  • Aggregated, anonymized data for research

We never sell your personal information to third parties.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request data deletion
  • Update your information
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications

Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies for authentication and session management. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated via email.

Contact Us

For privacy concerns or questions, contact us at: privacy@aquaguard.org
